Common Questions

How do I track my order?
Visit our Track Order page and enter your tracking ID or the email address you used at checkout.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns of unused products in original packaging within 7 days of delivery. Contact us with your order reference to begin the process.
How long does delivery take?
Uyo & Akwa Ibom: same or next day. South-South: 1–2 days. Nationwide: 3–5 business days. International: on request.
My payment failed but I was charged. What do I do?
Don't worry — failed payments are auto-reversed within 3–5 business days. Submit a ticket with your bank reference and we'll follow it up immediately.
Can I change or cancel my order?
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that, we may have already dispatched — call or WhatsApp us for fastest action.
